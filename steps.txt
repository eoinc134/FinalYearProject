Supervisors can supervise up to 7 students
Each session consists of 6 students and atleast 2 supervisors
Up to three sessions can be held per timeslot
There are 8 timeslots
Students must be in the same session as their supervisor
Supervisor availability for each timeslot is defined in a boolean array


Constraints:
T/S -> Students <= 6
T/S -> Supervisors > 2
Supervisor -> Students <= 7

Supervisor only scheduled when available
Supervisor can only appear in a timeslot once
Student must be in the same T/S as Supervisor
